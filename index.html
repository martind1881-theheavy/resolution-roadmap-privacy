Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

Simple Summary: Resolution Roadmap 2026 stores your goal data locally on your device and optionally syncs it to a secure cloud database. We never sell your data, and you have full control over it.

1. Information We Collect

Resolution Roadmap 2026 collects and stores the following information:

  • Resolution Data: Your chosen goals, custom tasks, and completion status
  • Progress Tracking: Task completion dates, streak counts, and statistics
  • API Keys: Your third-party AI provider credentials (stored locally and encrypted)
  • User Preferences: Settings like AI provider selection

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively for extension functionality:

  • Goal Management: Display your resolutions and track progress
  • AI Coaching: Generate personalized advice using your chosen AI provider
  • Cloud Sync: Optional backup to Supabase (encrypted connection)
  • Notifications: Remind you to complete daily tasks

3. Data Storage

Local Storage

All data is primarily stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your device unless you enable cloud sync.

Cloud Storage (Optional)

If cloud sync is enabled, your data is stored on Supabase servers with the following protections:

  • Encrypted HTTPS connections
  • No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected
  • You can delete your cloud data at any time using the "Reset Everything" button

4. Third-Party Services

AI Providers

When you use the AI Coach feature, your queries are sent to your chosen provider:

  • Google Gemini: Terms of Service
  • OpenAI (ChatGPT): Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic (Claude): Privacy Policy

Important: Your API keys are stored locally on your device. We never see or store your API credentials on our servers.

Supabase (Cloud Sync)

Our cloud sync feature uses Supabase for data backup. View their Privacy Policy.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Data is only transmitted to:

  • Your chosen AI provider (when you actively use the AI Coach feature)
  • Supabase servers (only if cloud sync is enabled)

7. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all stored data directly in the extension
  • Modify: Edit or update your goals and tasks at any time
  • Delete: Use the "Reset Everything" button to permanently delete all data
  • Export: Your data is stored in standard JSON format in Chrome's local storage

8. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS encryption for all network communications
  • Local-first architecture (data stays on your device by default)
  • No server-side data processing or analytics
  • API keys stored securely in Chrome's encrypted storage

9. Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in functionality or legal requirements. The "Last Updated" date will be revised accordingly. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Permissions Explained

Resolution Roadmap 2026 requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • storage: Save your goals and progress locally
  • alarms: Schedule daily reminder notifications
  • notifications: Display task reminders

We request only the minimum permissions necessary for core functionality.
